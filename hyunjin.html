Hwang Hyunjin

Hwang Hyunjin is the main dancer, rapper, and visual of Stray Kids. Born on March 20, 2000, he is well-loved for his charismatic stage presence, expressive
performances, and striking visuals. Hyunjin is also known for his artistic side, often sharing his love for drawing and creative expression with fans. His passion
and charm have made him one of the most popular members of the group.
